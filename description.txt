This simple mod adds unique sound effects to dealing critical damage with both melee and projectile-based weaponry!

Current features include:
• Randomized, unique sound effects for dealing and taking critical damage both with melee weaponry and projectiles,
• Categorized sound effects for all different projectiles!
• Original crit sounds for 8 different projectile categories (as well as stabbing with melee weaponry)!
• Easter eggs!
• Mod Settings Configurator (by Flashkirby99) support.

Changelog and extra download links are provided in the mod's linked forum post.
